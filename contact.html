FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Admissions open in January each year. Parents can collect the application form from the school office or download it from our website. After submitting the form along with required documents, students may be called for an interaction/assessment depending on the grade applied for.

School operates from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM for primary classes and 7:30 AM to 3:00 PM for secondary and higher secondary classes, Monday through Saturday. Half-day on Saturdays.

Yes, we provide bus transport covering all major routes in the city. GPS-tracked buses ensure student safety. Transport fees vary based on the distance from school.

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Students excelling in academics, sports, or arts are eligible for fee concessions. Contact our admissions office for more details.
