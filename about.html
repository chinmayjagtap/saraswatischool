About Our School

Three decades of excellence in education, nurturing generations of leaders and thinkers.

Our Story

A Journey of 35 Years

Founded in 1991 by visionary educationists, Saraswati High School began as a humble institution with just 50 students and 5 teachers. Named after Goddess Saraswati — the embodiment of knowledge, wisdom, and the arts — our school was born from a dream to make quality education accessible to all.

Over the decades, we have grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region. Today, with over 2,000 students and 120+ dedicated staff members, we continue to uphold the values that were laid down at our inception — integrity, excellence, compassion, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge.

Our campus has evolved from a single building to a sprawling 5-acre campus with modern infrastructure, including smart classrooms, science laboratories, a library, sports complex, and an auditorium that seats 500.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and stimulating environment that empowers every student to realize their unique potential, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society. We are committed to academic excellence, character building, and holistic development.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that sets benchmarks in academic achievement, innovation, and character development. We envision a school where every student is inspired to learn, grows to lead, and is empowered to make a meaningful difference in the world.

Our Values

Integrity, Respect, Compassion, Excellence, and Innovation form the cornerstone of everything we do. We believe that education goes beyond textbooks — it is about shaping character, fostering empathy, and building a community of lifelong learners.

Dr. Sunita Deshpande

Principal
Principal's Message

A Word From Our Principal

"Education is not merely about filling minds with information; it is about igniting the flame of curiosity, nurturing the seeds of compassion, and building the bridges of understanding that connect young hearts to the world."

At Saraswati High School, every day is an opportunity to learn something new — not just from books, but from each other, from nature, and from the rich tapestry of experiences that school life offers. I invite you to be part of our wonderful journey of learning, growth, and discovery.

— Dr. Sunita Deshpande, M.Ed., Ph.D.
