Our Story

A Journey of 27 Years

Founded in 2000 by Shri Suresh Vitthal Jadhav, Saraswati English Medium High School began as a primary school with a bold vision to provide quality English medium education in Chikhali, Pune. Named after Goddess Saraswati — the embodiment of knowledge, wisdom, and the arts — our school was born from a dream to make quality education accessible to all.

Over more than two decades, we have grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region. Today, with over 700 students and 35+ dedicated staff members, we continue to uphold the values that were laid down at our inception — integrity, excellence, compassion, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge.

Our campus spans 30 guntha (0.75 acres) with modern infrastructure, including smart classrooms, science laboratories, a library, and sports facilities. Affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board, we offer Primary, Secondary (SSC), and Higher Secondary (HSC) programs in Arts, Commerce, and Science streams.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and stimulating environment that empowers every student to realize their unique potential, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society. We are committed to academic excellence, character building, and holistic development.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that sets benchmarks in academic achievement, innovation, and character development. We envision a school where every student is inspired to learn, grows to lead, and is empowered to make a meaningful difference in the world.

Our Values

Integrity, Respect, Compassion, Excellence, and Innovation form the cornerstone of everything we do. We believe that education goes beyond textbooks — it is about shaping character, fostering empathy, and building a community of lifelong learners.
