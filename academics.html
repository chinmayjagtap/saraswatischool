Programs

Academic Programs

From nursery to 12th standard, we offer a structured pathway to academic excellence.

Primary
Grades 1-5

Primary School

Building strong foundations through activity-based learning, phonics, and creative exploration. Our primary program focuses on developing reading, writing, and arithmetic skills in a joyful environment.

  • Activity-Based Learning
  • English & Hindi & Marathi Medium
  • Art & Craft Sessions
  • Sports & Yoga
  • Value Education
Middle
Grades 6-8

Middle School

Expanding horizons with an enriched curriculum that includes sciences, mathematics, languages, and social studies. Students develop analytical and communication skills.

  • Science Laboratories
  • Computer Education
  • Third Language Option
  • Maharashtra State Board
  • Inter-School Competitions
High
Grades 9-12

High School

Preparing students for SSC and HSC board examinations and higher education with specialized streams — Science, Commerce, and Arts. Maharashtra State Board affiliated with rigorous academics combined with career guidance.

  • Science / Commerce / Arts
  • Board Exam Preparation
  • Career Counselling
  • Competitive Exam Coaching
  • Leadership Programs
